Do You Spend Time On the Internet?

I spend time on the Internet. Thanks to school, I spend a lot of time on the Internet. But I also use the Internet for other Internet-y things. Like finding the answers to questions, buying things I need (or want), reading the news ... or simply entertaining myself.

Whenever I get burned out braining for school things I usually pop over to one of my favorite sites to check out the comic for the day. You can check it out, too, at XKCD. I also keep track of the birds in my backyard through a large sliding glass door. This provides a good pause when working through a challenging programming assignment. We call it Birdy TV. The dogs love it.
